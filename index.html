About

We are the Cedarcrest Peer Tutoring Club. Founded in 2016, we aim to take an innovative approach towards the tutoring of students. Often, students struggle to receive one-on-one help from teachers due to time constraints and a perceived barrier in the expression of issues. This is where we come in. By having students tutor other students, we eliminate much of the response bias that exists in the ways students interact with teachers. Our philosophy is anchored on one key point: students remember the mistakes they make and the solutions they develop better than any formal instructor, and thus they are the best form of tutoring.
